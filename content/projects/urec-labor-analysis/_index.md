**What I did**
- Integrated scheduled and actual hours tables using SQL joins and data cleaning scripts.
- Built variance views for overall and negative-only discrepancies
- Identified recurring patterns to reduce wasted labor

**Results**
- ~$1K+ potential monthly savings
- Clearer audit trail and scheduling integrity
- Improved payroll accuracy and transparency across ~50 employees.

- SQL, Power BI, Excel, Python
